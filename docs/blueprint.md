# **App Name**: BilltriX

## Core Features:

- OTP Authentication & Business Onboarding: Secure sign-in via OTP with a detailed onboarding flow for business entities including GSTIN, PAN, and address setup.
- AI Intelligent Party Matcher Tool: Uses AI as a tool to suggest and validate parties and products from saved lists based on text matching and transaction history.
- Dynamic GST Invoice Engine: Supports sales and purchase invoices with integrated camera/scanner support and automated tax logic for SGST, CGST, and IGST based on state codes.
- Firestore Financial Database: Persistent, real-time ledger maintenance for BilltriX accounts, syncing receivable and payable balances across web and mobile platforms.
- Integrated Payment Vouchers: Create digital vouchers for receipts and payments with space for digital signatures and automatic tracking of transaction IDs.
- Comprehensive GSTR Reporting: Generates exportable PDF/Excel/CSV reports for GSTR-1 and GSTR-3B summaries, plus detailed product and sales reports.
- Inventory & Settings Console: Centralized management of party lists, product inventory adjustments, and customizable invoice format settings.

## Style Guidelines:

- The palette is centered on professional trust. Primary color is a deep, sophisticated Blue (#1E3A8A) for clear visibility.
- Background color is a soft, desaturated off-white with a hint of blue (#F1F5F9) to provide a clean environment for financial data.
- Accent color is a modern Royal Purple (#6D28D9), used for primary call-to-actions like 'Create Invoice'.
- Headline font is 'Space Grotesk' for a precise, tech-forward feel. Body text uses 'Inter' to maintain objective clarity for tabular data.
- Minimalistic line icons to distinguish between invoice types, payment modes, and reporting categories.
- Information-dense tabular layout optimized for ledger tracking and rapid line-item entry in invoices.
- Quick, purposeful transitions for switching between product entries and receipt generation tabs.
